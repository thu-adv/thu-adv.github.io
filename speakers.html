Course Instructor



Dai Jifeng

Dr. Dai Jifeng serves as Executive Research Director at SenseTime Research. His current research focus is on deep learning algorithms, especially for semantic segmentation and object detection in computer vision.
He has published more than 20 papers in world-renowned journals and conferences with more than 4800 citations. He won the 1st place in COCO challenge in 2015 and 2016. He is an Editorial Board Member of the IJCV and Senior PC Member of AAAI 2018.
He got his B.S. degree and Ph.D. degree from the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University in 2009 and 2014. During his Ph.D. study, he visited the VCLA lab of University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) between 2012 and 2013.


Liu Yu

Dr.Liu Yu is currently working as a vice research director in SenseTime Research. He received his PhD from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020, supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang. Li Hongyang

Dr. Li Hongyang is working as senior R&D manager of SenseTime. He is responsible for autonomous driving R&D related business. He received his PhD from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020, and received a full scholarship from the Hong Kong government.
